Assessing the risk of breast cancer using physical attributes from cell nuclei

In this report I used a Gaussian Copula model to identify characteristic relationships in the traits of cell nuclei where breast cancer was present. This method proved successful at predicting the presence of breast cancer cells with a surprising degree of accuracy.
