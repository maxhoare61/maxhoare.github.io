Assessing the risk of breast cancer using physical attributes from cell nuclei

I prepared this report for the Bayesian Statistics course at ANU, applying a Gaussian Copula model to analyse cell nucleus attributes from a University of California Irvine dataset. The model successfully identified characteristic relationships and distinguished benign from cancerous cells with high accuracy.
